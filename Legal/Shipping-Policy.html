We offer free worldwide delivery and free returns on all orders via premium service couriers.

Delivery timings

Europe: UPS Express shipping - Delivery 2-3 working days
Asia, Middle East: UPS Express shipping - Delivery 3-5 working days
America, Oceania: UPS Express shipping - Delivery 2-4 working days
Africa: UPS Express shipping - Delivery 2-4 working days

Please note that our warehouse is closed during holidays.

All deliveries are climate compensated through our courier to minimize the climate impact of our transports.

After the order has been dispatched from our warehouse you will receive an email & SMS with the tracking ID from our courier. Our courier delivers from Monday to Friday during business hours and will make three delivery attempts. After the third failed attempt, your order will be returned to our warehouse.

Your order will be delivered directly to the address you have selected during check-out. If you wish to change the delivery address or switch to a different delivery option more convenient to you, you may do so for free by following the link in your SMS.

FAQ

  • Where do you ship my order from?
    We ship all our orders from our warehouse in Sweden.

  • Can I choose a delivery date/time?
    You can change the delivery date of your shipment through the SMS link provided by UPS. If you want to choose a specific time for the delivery you can contact UPS customer service (www.ups.com) with your tracking ID.

  • What should I do if my order has not been delivered yet?
    If your order has not been delivered within the estimated period, please first check the order status by using the tracking ID provided in the SMS from our courier. For further assistance, please contact us, we will be happy to assist you.
